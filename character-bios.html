Iron Man

Tony Stark, aka Iron Man, is a billionaire industrialist and genius inventor who is the armored superhero of the MCU.

Captain America

Steve Rogers, aka Captain America, is the super-soldier who fights for justice with his iconic shield.

Thor

Thor is the Asgardian God of Thunder, wielding the mighty hammer Mjolnir.
